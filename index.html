About Me

Jonah Zane is a 15-year-old singer/songwriter who lives in New York's Hudson Valley. He has loved to write his whole life. Jonah studies classical violin, piano, and guitar, but any instrument he sees he will find a way to play it! His music mainly lives in the pop territory but he sometimes ventures into rock 'n' roll and hip hop.
